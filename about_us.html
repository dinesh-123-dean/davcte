About DAV CTE Koraput

Our Mission

DAV College of Teacher Education, Koraput aims to prepare competent, compassionate, and innovative educators for tomorrow's classrooms. We focus on developing both academic and ethical values in our future teachers.

Vision

To be a leading institution in teacher education through commitment to excellence in teaching, learning, and community service.

History

Established with the goal of addressing the educational needs of the region, DAV CTE Koraput has been shaping the future of teacher training with consistent dedication and innovation.
